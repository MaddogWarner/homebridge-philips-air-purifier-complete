Connecting…
Advanced: log in via browser
  1. Open the Philips login page (a new tab will open automatically — click the link if it didn't).
  2. Log in to your Philips account and approve access when prompted.
  3. After approving, your browser will try to open the Philips Air app and will land on either an error page or a Philips page (accounts.home.id) — this is expected.
  4. Copy the full URL from your browser's address bar and paste it below. It works whether it starts with com.philips.air://loginredirect?code=… or is an accounts.home.id address — as long as it contains code=….
